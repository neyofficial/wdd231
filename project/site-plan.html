Individual Project Site Plan

Personal Finance & Career Growth Hub for Young Professionals

Site Name

Site Name: ThrivePath

This name represents growth, direction, and intentional progress in both financial and career development for young professionals.

Optional Domain: thrivepath.org

Site Purpose

The website provides practical, easy-to-understand resources on personal finance and early-career development. Content includes budgeting tips, saving strategies, financial planning basics, CV writing guidance, job search strategies, and professional growth advice for students and young professionals.

Scenarios

  • How can I create a simple budget as a young professional with a limited income?
  • What steps can I take to improve my CV and increase my chances of getting a job?

Color Scheme

The selected colors support clarity, professionalism, and trust.

  • Deep Blue (#1f3a5f): Headings, navigation, and accents
  • Light Gray (#f4f6f8): Page background
  • Soft Green (#3fa796): Buttons, highlights, and links

Typography

  • Inter (Sans-serif): Used for headings and body text for clarity and readability
